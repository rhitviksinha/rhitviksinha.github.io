California IMAX Map

When Interstellar was re-released, I wanted to find where IMAX 70mm theatres were in California, which took some of searching. Then Project Hail Mary came out and I found myself doing the same thing again. Amaze. Amaze. Amaze.

So now, I have an interactive SVG map of every IMAX theatre in California. IMAX data from IMAX Fandom Wiki. California SVG map sourced from PublicaMundi MappingAPI.

Filter by projector type (GT Laser, CoLa, Single Laser), aspect ratio, and theatre chain. Zoom into the Bay Area, Greater LA, or San Diego. Click or hover venues for screen dimensions and specs.
